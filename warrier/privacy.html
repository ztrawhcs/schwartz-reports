WarrierBot Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

WarrierBot is a private, single-user AI assistant operated by Matthew Schwartz for personal use only. It is not a commercial service and does not send messages to any third parties.

What data is collected

SMS messages sent to and from the WarrierBot phone number are logged locally on the operator's personal server for the purpose of maintaining conversation continuity. No data is transmitted to any third party except Twilio (the SMS carrier) and Anthropic (the AI provider), both of which have their own privacy policies.

How data is used

Message history is used solely to provide context to the AI assistant during conversations. It is not used for marketing, advertising, or any purpose other than the personal use of the operator.

Data sharing

No personal data is sold, rented, or shared with any third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.
