WarrierBot Terms and Conditions

Last updated: March 2026

WarrierBot is a private, single-user AI assistant operated by Matthew Schwartz for personal use. This service is not open to the public.

Program description

WarrierBot sends and receives SMS messages on behalf of its sole operator. Message frequency varies based on operator use. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Opt-out

To stop receiving messages, reply STOP to any message. To resume, reply START.
