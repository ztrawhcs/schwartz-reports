I'm a journalist covering law and policy at Bloomberg Law (Bloomberg Industry Group). I host and produce UnCommon Law, Bloomberg Law's flagship narrative audio program, which has won the ABA Silver Gavel Award for excellence in legal journalism.

UnCommon Law tells the stories behind the biggest legal questions of our time — stories about the people, institutions, and ideas that shape American law.

Before Bloomberg, I worked in public radio. I'm based in the Washington, D.C. area.
